# Privacy Policy for Read Aloud Extension

**Last Updated: May 28, 2026**

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the **Read Aloud Extension** handles data and information when you use our Chrome extension.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Read Aloud Extension is designed to protect your privacy. 

* **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, identifiers, or sensitive user information.
* **No Tracking:** We do not track your browsing history, your search queries, or your usage habits.
* **No Third-Party Sharing:** Since we do not collect any data, no information is shared with or sold to third parties.

---

## 2. Local Storage Usage

The extension uses Chrome's `storage` API (`chrome.storage.local`) solely to store your preferences on your own device. This includes:
* Your voice playback settings (voice selection, speed, pitch, volume).
* UI preferences (such as light/dark mode and interface theme).
* The current reading position of the last document read (so you can resume where you left off).

This data never leaves your device and is not synchronized or shared with any external servers.

---

## 3. Text-to-Speech Processing

All Text-to-Speech (TTS) processing is performed locally on your device:
* The extension extracts text from the web page or PDF you are viewing using Chrome's standard APIs (`activeTab` and `scripting`).
* The text is read aloud using Chrome's native Speech Synthesis engine (`chrome.tts` or `window.speechSynthesis`).
* The text content of the pages you read is **never** uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any external servers.

---

## 4. Third-Party Sites and Services

When you use the extension on third-party websites or read online PDFs, their respective privacy policies apply to those web pages. The Read Aloud Extension does not interact with those websites beyond extracting the text on your screen to read it aloud.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
